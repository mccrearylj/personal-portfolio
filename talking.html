Talking to Myself


Collaborator(s): Nicholas Burkhalter

'Talking to Myself' is a publication composed of the stories and unique lives of six individuals across ASU's campus. We conducted a series of interviews and wrote editorials about our experiences and relationships to the topics discussed.

This publication aids the interviewed students in realizing positive aspects about themselves, as well as relate to people around campus in all different aspects of life, and to us as the writers.
