ASU Football Video Department


Type of work // internship

Appalachian State University's football team has a dedicated staff of videographers and talented designers. This team is split into Coaching Staff and Production Team; each side is responsible for certain tasks, including but not limited to creating promotional and recruiting materials, motivational game videos, and the general upkeep of the football team's marketing strategies and graphical presence, both within the university and outside.
