Multicultural Student Development


Type of work // student employment

The Office of Multicultural Student Development celebrates diversity on Appalachian State University's campus. This main office hub encompasses the LGBT Center, Women's Center and Multicultural Center on campus, as well as a wide variety of multicultural and diverse clubs.

As Project Assistant, I am responsible for all graphics materials produced for promoting events, clubs, panels, etc. I also upkeep the website (multicultural.appstate.edu) using Drupal, and the office Scala Digital Signage on two TVs in the ASU Plemmons Student Union.
