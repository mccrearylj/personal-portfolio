The Life of Serge Gainsbourg


Type of work // independent study

Independent Study with Mark Nystrom, 'iPad Publication Design.' Assignment was to create an iPad documentary via an interactive iPad publication. Topic chosen was Serge Gainsbourg, infamous French singer, songwriter and actor. Publication has a two-direction navigation with five main sections and a bibliography.
