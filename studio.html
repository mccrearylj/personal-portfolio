Graphic Design Senior Studio Portfolio

Displayed below are the final projects required as part of the capstone course for the Graphic Design Department at Appalachian State University. Each student is required to thoroughly research information to use in a total of four projects. Of these projects, one must be branding of a local non-profit or business of any nature, and one must be a collaboration with other students.
