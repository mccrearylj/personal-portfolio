Tattoo Me (branding)


Collaborator(s):
Jake Flowers, Logan Hall

Tattoo Me is an upscale tattoo shop that will be opening in the Plaza Midwood area in Charlotte at the end of 2015. We were approached by the lead tattoo artist, who wanted a branding system that would label the shop as inviting to people who want a tattoo but also to the artistic community of Charlotte.

Our end goal is to inject tattoos as another media of art for the city.
