Defensei


Collaborator(s): n/a

Defensei is an Instagram account filled with illustrations of self-defense moves that anyone can learn in their free time; it also gives information about staying safe and protecting yourself in your home and life.

This social media account, easily accessible from anywhere, will help students across campus in ensuring a better sense of safety, knowing that they can learn these simple modes of self-defense and keep them in their heads if the situation were to arise.
