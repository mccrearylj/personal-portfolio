How does the competition home fit in?

Economic justification

Maison Reciprocity is innovative on many fronts and promotes both economic and environmental stability. Incorporating contemporary design, sustainable features, high­tech materials, and a sophisticated urban sensibility. The Maison Reciprocity row­house concept is flexible in design in order to be responsive to the extreme diversity of regional situations.

The Maison Reciprocity residential scheme creates a building that fits the scale of the existing townscape. The design concept identifies opportunities for economic and social growth, as well as, preserves and enhances the traditional character of prescribed spaces. The Maison Reciprocity design captures urban industrial sleekness with rural soul.

With an estimated payback period of 4 to 6 years depending on final production model product choices and annual energy savings of nearly $4,000 a year, Maison Reciprocity offers an energy­efficient, affordable alternative to standard construction models. Production model cost estimates for construction range from $125 to $175 per square foot.
