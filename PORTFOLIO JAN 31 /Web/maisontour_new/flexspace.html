Energy Efficient Window/Door Frames

Ply Gem Low­E glass does more than just lower energy bills. It delivers comfort, creates less condensation and protects valuables from sun damage far better than conventional clear glass. Plus, it meets specific solar and energy control needs for different climates and geographic regions nationwide.

Heat Mirror Windows and Doors

Heat Mirror is an extremely high performing, energy efficient glass system, which dramatically improves thermal insulation and indoor comfort, compared to standard insulating glass (IG) units. The technology suspends up to three films in the airspace of a single insulating glass unit to create multiple air tight chambers that deliver best in class insulation performance for windows. Standard insulating glass with low­e coatings delivers thermal insulating performance (or R Value) of 3 while Heat Mirror insulating glass can achieve an R Value up to 20
